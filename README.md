Netlify AI Prompt – Site Structure for christinefwalker.com

Build a modern personal website for Christine Walker, a connector, real estate professional, and community builder based in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Christine operates at the intersection of hospitality, creative collaboration, entrepreneurship, and real estate development. She helps bring people, ideas, and spaces together to create meaningful projects and partnerships.

The website should feel like a modern creative studio / strategic connector platform, not a typical personal blog or influencer site.

The aesthetic should be minimal, warm, and editorial, using natural tones such as terracotta, sage, sand, and warm neutrals with generous white space.

Avoid corporate design styles or overly trendy influencer visuals.

Primary Goal of the Website

The website should help visitors quickly understand:

• Who Christine is
• What kinds of projects she facilitates
• What she is building in St. Petersburg
• How people can collaborate with her

The site should emphasize clarity, trust, and thoughtful leadership.

Target Audience

Primary visitors include:

• creators and entrepreneurs
• collaborators and partners
• real estate investors and developers
• community leaders
• people exploring new business or creative projects

The site should make Christine appear as someone who connects the right people to the right opportunities.

Site Structure

Generate a clean, modern layout with the following sections:

1. Hero Section

Headline should communicate Christine’s role as a connector and project facilitator, not just a job title.

Example tone:

"I connect people, projects, and spaces to help ideas move forward."

Include two call-to-action buttons:

• Start a Conversation
• Explore Current Projects

Include a subtle trust element such as:

St. Petersburg based | Hospitality + Real Estate Background

2. About Christine

Explain Christine’s background in:

• Human Resource Management and Hospitality & Tourism (Virginia Tech)
• Real estate and development in the St. Petersburg area
• community building and collaboration

Focus on how her work centers on helping people transition into new chapters, whether that is a new city, new project, or new business idea.

Keep the tone thoughtful and grounded.

3. Project Ecosystem

Show the categories of work Christine is involved in.

Example categories:

• Real Estate & Development
• Sacred Space Studios (creative collaboration environment)
• Creator Interviews & Conversations
• Community Projects and Events

Each should have a short description explaining the purpose of the project.

This section should feel like a hub of initiatives rather than separate businesses.

4. Conversation & Thought Leadership

Introduce Christine’s interview and conversation work, including structured interviews with creators, entrepreneurs, and builders.

Explain that Christine enjoys hosting conversations that explore:

• how people build things
• how ideas become projects
• the thinking behind meaningful work

This can include articles, interviews, or future podcast/video content.

5. Collaboration Opportunities

Explain how people can work with Christine.

Examples:

• project collaboration
• speaking or hosting conversations
• development partnerships
• creative collaborations

Include a contact form or email CTA.

6. Location & Community

Highlight St. Petersburg, Florida as the home base.

Explain Christine’s belief that place and environment shape collaboration and growth.

Tone Guidelines

Writing should feel:

• intelligent
• calm and confident
• thoughtful
• human

Avoid:

• buzzwords
• hype language
• influencer-style messaging
• overly spiritual language

The voice should feel like someone who hosts meaningful conversations and builds thoughtful projects.

Visual Direction

Design should feel:

• minimal
• editorial
• modern
• warm

Use:

• large typography
• neutral color palette
• soft textures
• high-quality imagery

The site should feel similar to a creative studio, boutique consultancy, or modern cultural organization.

Key Message

Visitors should leave the site understanding:

Christine Walker brings people, ideas, and environments together to help meaningful projects take shape.
