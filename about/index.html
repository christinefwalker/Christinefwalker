About

Environment shapes behavior. The spaces we live, work, and create in influence performance, clarity, relationships, and identity.

I'm Christine — a connector, strategist, and ecosystem builder. My primary paid work helps founder-led businesses design the people, positioning, and operating rhythm behind a high-integrity sales team.

The flagship engagement is a focused 90-day Sales Team Foundation, starting at $7,500. Remote income, hospitality, real estate, relocation, and wellness shape how I think about freedom and environment; they support the work rather than compete with it.

I also connect brands with aligned partners, design experiences and spaces where meaningful things happen, and write about the patterns that shape how people build and grow.
